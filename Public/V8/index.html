About

Who We Are

    All is Well is an environmental organization in Kakinada focused on promoting cleanliness and green cover through tree plantation and public awareness campaigns. The organization, founded by N. Kishore Kumar and a group of friends, has planted around 23,000 saplings in Kakinada and surrounding villages. We work in close partnership with the Kakinada Municipal Corporation and the Forest Department to advance our environmental and community initiatives. All is Well also encourages public participation in keeping the city clean and green through initiatives like public policing and waste management awareness.

    Imagine our beloved hometown lush with vegetation, roads canopied with trees and parks rich with flora and fauna. It was from such an imagination, All Is Well was born in 2014.

    We believe in bringing a revolution in our city - one which ensures everyone contributes to making our city's tomorrow better than yesterday. To mobilize, direct and succeed in this objective, lies our purpose. For nearly a decade, dozens of All is Well members and volunteers, collaborating with wide variety of partners, have contributed to this dream, inching towards making our city healthy, sustainable and green with our 3Hours for Society initiative.

Our Works

  • Over 300 Avenue and Block Plantation drives.
  • Over 150 Cleanliness and Beautification drives in City.
  • COVID-19 relief by awareness generation, mask and essentials distribution, supply of oxygen concentrators.
  • Beach cleaning, Olive Ridley Habitat restoration.
  • Urban Park cleaning and Maintenance.
  • Community Engagement programs through workshops, communal Kitchen gardens, awareness campaigns.
  • Rescue operations during natural disasters, animal rescues.
  • Swachh Bharat collaborations with Government.
  • Free home service for old aged bed ridden pensioners for submission of life certificate.
  • Main road tree plantation from Masjid center to Sarpavaram center.
  • Swatch Bharat collaboration with KMC.
  • Gardening Workshops and Kitchen Garden establishment in ASD Women's college, Jagannaickpur.
  • Parks revival, greening and maintenance project.
